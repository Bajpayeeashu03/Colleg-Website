COURSES WE OFFER

The College has created an enviable track record of imparting excellent education to the aspiring engineers and managers.
Under the aegis of Kalyan Bharti Trust, HITK endeavors to achieve academic excellence through better teaching and a research oriented approach for the young intellectuals to prepare for their leadership roles in a competitive world.

B.TECH

Bachelor of Technology is an Undergraduate Four-year Engineering Course which is offered in various Disciplines and Specialisations. Admission is done on the basis of scores in Various National & State-Level Entrance Exams.

M.TECH

Master of Technology is a Professional Postgraduate Engineering Programme awarded to candidates after completion of Two years of study in the Discipline of Engineering. HITK has Excellent Faculty Members, Hostel, Library & Laboratory Facilities.

MCA

Master of Computer Application is a Two-year Postgraduate Course that trains students in the various aspects of Computer Programs, Application Software, Computer Architecture, 0perating Systems & many more.

OUR FACILITIES

We Provide The Best Facilities In Our College. It Includes : Central Computing Facilities, Banking, Health Care, Transportation, Cafeteria, Hostel, Sports, Smart Classes, Library, Laboratory And Many more.

CAFETERIA

The Institute has an excellent cafeteria with a seating capacity for more than 200 persons. It has a well-planned hygienic kitchen run by a reputed caterer at administered prices. Multi-cuisine lunch and snacks are being served in the cafeteria. A fast food restaurant-cum-convenience store is also there.

SPORTS/GAMES

A common room, equipped with major indoor games facilities like Table Tennis, Carom and Chess are available. There are excellent facilities for outdoor games and sports like cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, golf and swimming managed by a Sports Officer and a Swimming Instructor.

CENTRAL LIBRARY

The library is centrally air-conditioned and equipped with computers and communication network and has a seating capacity of 250 readers. This is equipped with CCTV, Antitheft Solution system and also with Wi-Fi connectivity. The library is computerized with LIBSYS library management software, with barcode technology.
